ABOUT US

Bean Around Town is your go-to guide for discovering the top coffee spots in Pampanga. We're passionate about promoting local coffee culture and helping you find the perfect café that matches your vibe.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is simple: to shine a spotlight on the unique, local coffee shops that deserve recognition. We believe that every coffee shop has its own story, vibe, and specialty, and we're here to help you find the one that feels like home.

HOW WE STARTED

Bean Around Town began with a simple love for coffee and a desire to explore more than just the mainstream cafés. Thus, we decided to create this guide to highlight the best local coffee shops, share their stories, and help others discover the perfect spot.

WHY LOCAL?

By supporting local coffee shops, you're not just enjoying great coffee you're also helping small businesses thrive, contributing to the local economy, and preserving the city's unique coffee culture.
